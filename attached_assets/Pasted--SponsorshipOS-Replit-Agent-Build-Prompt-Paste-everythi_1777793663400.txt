## PRODUCT OVERVIEW

**SponsorshipOS** is an AI-powered back-office operating system for mid-tier creators and creator agencies.

**Positioning:** "Stripe + Notion + AI contract ops for creators."

**Problem:** Creators lose income because sponsorship contracts are scattered across PDFs, emails, DMs, invoices, and calendars. They miss deadlines, forget deliverables, under-invoice brands, fail to reserve tax money, and cannot prove performance to sponsors.

**Solution:** SponsorshipOS turns a sponsorship agreement into an automated operational workflow — from contract upload through AI extraction, task generation, Kanban management, compliance verification, invoicing, and reporting.

**Target users:** Mid-tier YouTube/TikTok/Instagram creators, micro-influencers, newsletter creators, creator managers, small creator agencies.

### 10. TAX RESERVE CALCULATOR

**UI:** `/app/tax`

Every time an invoice is created or marked paid, calculate and save to `tax_reserves`:

```
grossAmount = invoice.amount

netAmount = grossAmount - platformFee
reserveAmount = netAmount * (userProfile.taxReservePercent / 100)
availableBalance = netAmount - reserveAmount
```
